# Governance

OpenAgentic is an open-source project, published under the Apache License 2.0
and stewarded by **Agenticwork™ LLC**.

## Decision-making

The project follows a maintainer-led governance model. Agenticwork LLC and the
maintainers listed in [MAINTAINERS.md](MAINTAINERS.md) hold final authority
over:

- the technical roadmap and architecture,
- what is accepted into the `main` branch,
- releases, and
- the project's trademarks and brand (see [TRADEMARK.md](TRADEMARK.md)).

Day to day, decisions are made by consensus among maintainers in issues and
pull requests. Where consensus is not reached, the lead maintainer decides.

## Contributions

Anyone may propose changes via pull request (see
[CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md)). **Every contribution is reviewed by a
maintainer and is merged solely at the maintainers' discretion.** `main` is
branch-protected; opening a pull request does not entitle it to be merged.

All participation is governed by [CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md).
