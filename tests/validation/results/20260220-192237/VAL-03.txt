## 1. FedRAMP AI Prioritization Program (Effective August 18, 2025)

FedRAMP has launched a **dedicated AI prioritization track** under the **20x program**, fast-tracking authorization for conversational AI cloud services used by federal agencies. This was formally triggered by a **CIO Council letter (August 12, 2025)** and **FedRAMP Board electronic vote**.

All five criteria must be met simultaneously:

| # | Requirement | Category |

| 2 | **Data separation guarantee** — customer training data cannot leave customer environment | Data Protection |
| 3 | **Demand signal** — ≥5 CFO Act agencies OR CIO Council recommendation | Market Validation |
| 4 | **GSA MAS availability** — listed on GSA Multiple Award Schedule | Acquisition |
| 5 | **20x readiness** — meet FedRAMP 20x requirements within **2 months** of acceptance | Speed |
